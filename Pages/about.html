About Us - ASOCORP

At ASOCORP, we design, manufacture, and deliver innovative smart systems that make everyday living smarter, safer, and more efficient in Africa. From automatic timers to home automation solutions and remote-controlled systems for offices, we are dedicated to transforming the way people interact with their environments.

Our mission is not just about creating devices; it’s about creating experiences that help you focus on what truly matters — whether that’s spending more time with loved ones, running a more efficient office, or simply enjoying the peace of knowing your environment is intelligently managed.

At ASOCORP, we have a dedicated team of innovators, engineers, and visionaries who are passionate about driving change through innovation. We blend modern engineering with user-friendly design to create products that integrate seamlessly into everyday life. Whether it's automating your home, managing office operations, or introducing intelligent control systems to homes and offices in Africa, ASOCORP is committed to delivering precise and easy-to-use solutions.

With a focus on excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we are not just building smart systems — we are shaping a company built on trust, innovation, and excellence, ASOCORP is more than a brand — it’s a partner in your journey to a smarter way of living and working.
